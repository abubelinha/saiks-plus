Usage

Select character states from the table at the left. As you make selections, taxa in the list on the right will turn red to indicate that they do not match your selections. Also, as you select character states, items in the characteristics table will grey out to indicate that they are no longer possible (no taxa would have the resulting combination of character states). When you select a most typical character state (dark green) the corresponding taxa will also turn dark green in the green color list, indicating that this is probably the taxa to be identified. Select a character state only when you are confident. If not, do not select.

If you click on a taxon name in the table on the right, you will be taken to the web page for this taxon.

If you click on "SHOW CHARS" next to a taxon name, the character states of that taxon will be shown in the characteristics table. When the taxa has typical character states, they will be indicated by a dark green color.

If you click on the "RESET" button at top left, all characters will be reset.

If you click on the name of a character, that character will be reset.
